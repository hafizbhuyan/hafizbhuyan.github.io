Drivense

The Beginning

Drivense was a product of the mix of being a frustrated consumer and wanting to build something from scratch. During the spring semester of my freshman year, I was part of, and now lead, an organization called the Society of Entrepreneurs. This was a group made up of student entrepreneurs, all with active businesses, turning their passions into paychecks. Over the summer of 2018, I wanted to build something of my own, and while looking for my assigned textbooks, I got confused with the high prices. After a few days, while I was listening to Spotify the gears in my head started turning. How can Netflix and Spotify host millions of movies, tv shows and music and charge just a few dollars per month? I decided to apply the concept behind those two companies to college textbooks and Drivense was born.

2019 JMU Summer Accelerator

Drivense was accepted into the 2019 JMU Summer Accelerator. During the 3 months, the company joined 7 other startups from JMU to collaborate on ideas and solve problems together for each of our companies. We were also given a $4,000 stipend and given invaluable mentorship on the Lean Startup, Doings Things That Don't Scale, Branding, building a Mean Viable Product(MVP), and pitching our companies. At the end of this, on September, each company was given the opportunity to pitch in front of an audience of JMU's President, College Deans, the Provost, and notable alumni. It's hard to describe in words what the experience was like, but it was invaluable.
